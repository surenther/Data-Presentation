Data Story Definition¶

Audience, Purpose, and Medium

The audience is Car Insurance Executives who need to make high-stakes decisions on policy pricing. The purpose is to demonstrate that the 2020 security exploit has created a "New Era" of risk that requires immediate underwriting changes. The medium is a Jupyter Notebook PDF, which provides a transparent, data-backed narrative.

Design Strategy and Gestalt Principles

  • Similarity: I used "Crisis Red" for all Kia/Hyundai data to create an immediate mental link between that color and high risk.

  • Proximity: Stacked visuals place the red "at-risk" data directly against the "Other" data to show the physical encroachment of this trend.

  • Sizing: I increased the plot dimensions to ensure that even though some smaller cities (like Winnemucca) have small boxes, the overall scale of the crisis is unmistakable.

Ethical Considerations

  • Transformations: I cleaned the carTheftsMap.csv by removing commas and formatting percentages as numeric values to ensure calculations were accurate.

  • Assumptions: I assumed the brands "Kia" and "Hyundai" primarily represent the un-immobilized models, as specific trim data was unavailable.

  • Redlining Risk: To avoid unfairly penalizing residents based on their city, I advocate for software-based security tiers rather than simple geographic rate hikes.

  • Credibility: The data is grounded in Motherboard's investigative sets and verified PD records, providing a solid ethical foundation for the recommendations.
